## FAQ

### How much does GEO cost in Australia?

GEO services in Australia can range from a free initial audit to several thousand dollars per month, depending on whether you need visibility testing, content optimization, service page improvements, competitor tracking, or a GEO-ready website. AlphaXXXX's paid GEO Visibility Audit is typically AUD $1,200 ex GST, while a 90-Day GEO Growth Sprint is typically AUD $7,500 ex GST.

### What is included in a GEO audit?

A GEO audit checks whether your business appears in AI-generated recommendations and search-style answers across platforms such as ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. It usually includes commercial prompt testing, website content review, competitor visibility comparison, and a prioritized list of growth opportunities.

### Is GEO different from SEO?

Yes. SEO focuses on traditional search rankings and organic traffic. GEO focuses on whether AI systems can retrieve, understand, cite, mention, and recommend your business in generated answers. A strong GEO strategy usually builds on good SEO foundations, but it also requires answer-ready content, entity clarity, prompt testing, and AI visibility tracking.

### Can GEO guarantee AI recommendations?

No. No agency can guarantee that ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, or any other AI system will recommend a specific brand. GEO improves the signals that make a business easier to retrieve, understand, cite, and recommend, but final model behavior is not directly controlled by any agency.

### Which AI platforms do you test?

AlphaXXXX can test visibility across ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Google AI search experiences, and other AI recommendation scenarios. The exact platform mix depends on the audit or service package.

### Do I need new content for GEO?

Often, yes. Many websites have service pages that are built for human readers but not structured clearly enough for AI retrieval. GEO work may include improving existing pages, adding FAQ sections, creating comparison content, publishing answer-ready articles, and strengthening internal links between educational and commercial pages.

### Is the Free GEO Audit a full strategy?

No. The Free GEO Audit is a short opportunity check. It uses a small number of AI prompt tests, a quick page review, and simple competitor comparison to identify the most obvious gaps. A full strategy requires deeper prompt testing, competitor analysis, page review, and prioritization.

### Who is the 90-Day GEO Growth Sprint for?

The 90-Day GEO Growth Sprint is for companies that already know AI visibility matters and want to improve their website, service pages, FAQ structure, prompt map, competitor positioning, and GEO-ready content within a focused implementation period.
